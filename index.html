New to Anime!

Here are a few shows that I hope you are interested in!

One Piece

This show is about Pirates. It is a story about a guy name Monkey D. Luffy and his dream of becoming King of the Pirates. In order to become the King of the Pirates he needs to find the One Piece, a famous treasure that used to belong to Gold Roger who was the original king of the pirate. This show is also known for its world building since when there is a lot of lore that happens that make the viewers speculate on what this world's secrets can hold. The anime shows us about themes like Friendship, Dreams, and more importantly Freedom.

Dragon Ball Z

This show is the anime. It is one of the most iconic anime of all time. It is a legendary tale of a man named Goku who wanted to be the strongest martial artist in the world. This show is mostly how Goku and his friends protect the earth from villains like Frieza, Cell, and Majin Buu. For me this show mostly tells me that nothing is impossible since in this show both Goku and Vegeta would always train to become stronger and stronger to surpass their limits.

Demon Slayer

This anime takes place in the Taisho era in Japan. It's about a boy name Tanjiro who is finding a cure to make her sister into human because she was turned into a demon by a man named Muzan. Tanjiro is in a organization called the Demon Slayer Corps where there are people who want to take out demons who wanted to eat humans. This show is mostly how a person's kidness can make a difference on a person.
